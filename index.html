Impact of Climate Change on Storm Frequency

Climate change has led to increased frequency and intensity of severe weather events across the United States. Rising global temperatures create conditions favorable for more powerful storms.

Conclusion

Scientists predict storm patterns will continue to change as global warming progresses. Understanding historical trends helps communities prepare for future weather challenges.
